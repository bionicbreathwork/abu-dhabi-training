The Bionic Curriculum

Everything you need to teach & thrive

01
Introduction & Foundations of Bionic Breathwork

Overview of Breathwork: history, principles, and benefits. Understanding the role of a Bionic Breathwork teacher. Ethical considerations and guidelines for safe facilitation. Establishing a safe and supportive environment — contraindications.

02
Theory and Techniques

Theoretical foundations of Bionic Breathwork: integrating neuroscience, human biology, psychology, transpersonal theory, and spirituality. Core techniques and practices. Exploring the importance of non-ordinary states of consciousness.

03
Delivering a Bionic Breathwork Session

Preparation and guidelines for breathwork sessions. Demonstrations of the breathwork process. Holding Space. Cacao — heart opening ceremony. Group sharing and integration exercises. Q&A and clarification of session experiences.

04
Participant Dynamics and Challenges

Understanding various participant types and their needs. Addressing challenging emotions and experiences during breathwork. Group dynamics and conflict resolution strategies.

05
Ethics

Professional standards for Bionic Breathwork facilitation. Informed consent and confidentiality. Ethical dilemmas and decision-making processes.

06
The Bionic Breathwork Business Blueprint

Certification requirements and expectations. Next steps: building a career as a Breathwork facilitator. Planning and structuring a Breathwork workshop. The business of wellness retreats. Social Media (Meta) Ad tutorials. SEO (search engine optimization). Creating rituals and sacred space. Group facilitation skills and techniques.
